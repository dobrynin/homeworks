Buffalo Chicken Dip
  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

"This tangy, creamy dip tastes just like Buffalo chicken wings. It's best served hot with crackers and celery sticks. Everyone loves the results!" Buffalo wing

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • cream cheese
  • Ranch
  • Pepper sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Celery
  • Crackers

Directions

  1. Heat chicken and hot sauce in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Serve with celery sticks and crackers.
